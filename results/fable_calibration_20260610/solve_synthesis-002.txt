Construct the strongest possible case FOR the monorepo (4 arguments), then the strongest possible case AGAINST it, i.e. for polyrepo (4 arguments). Each side must address:
- CI/CD pipeline design
- Code sharing and dependency management
- Team autonomy
- Developer onboarding

Then provide a balanced recommendation that honestly acknowledges what the organization would be giving up with either choice.

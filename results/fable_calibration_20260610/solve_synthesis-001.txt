A fintech startup processing 50,000 transactions per second needs to choose a data consistency strategy for their distributed payment system. Compare these three approaches:

(a) Two-phase commit (2PC)
(b) Saga pattern with compensating transactions
(c) Event sourcing with CQRS

For each approach, analyse:
- Transaction throughput under load
- Consistency guarantees (and their limits)
- Failure recovery behavior
- Operational complexity

Conclude with a specific recommendation for this use case, justified by the analysis above.

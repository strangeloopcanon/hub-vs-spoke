Five friends — Alice, Bob, Carol, Dave, and Eve — each own exactly one pet (cat, dog, fish, hamster, parrot) and live on the same street in houses numbered 1 through 5 from left to right.

Constraints:
1. Alice lives in house 1.
2. The dog owner lives directly next to the cat owner.
3. Eve lives in an even-numbered house.
4. The fish owner lives in house 3.
5. Bob lives immediately to the right of Carol.
6. Dave does not own the parrot.
7. The hamster owner lives in an odd-numbered house.
8. Carol does not live next to Alice.
9. Eve owns neither the dog nor the fish.
10. The parrot owner lives in a higher-numbered house than the dog owner.
11. Bob does not own the cat.

Determine the complete assignment: who lives in which house and owns which pet. Show your deductive reasoning step by step. There is exactly one valid solution.

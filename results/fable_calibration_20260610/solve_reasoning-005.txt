You have a 3×3 grid. Place the digits 1 through 9 (each used exactly once) in the grid so that:

1. Each row sums to 15
2. Each column sums to 15
3. Both main diagonals sum to 15
4. The top-left cell contains 2
5. The center cell contains 5

Provide the completed grid and prove it is the ONLY solution satisfying all five constraints. Your proof must show why no other placement works — don't just exhibit one solution.
